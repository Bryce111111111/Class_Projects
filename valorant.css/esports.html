WHAT IS ESPORTS ABOUT

Valorant esports is a competitive scene where professional teams from around the world compete in tournaments for prize money and prestige. Organized by Riot Games through the Valorant Champions Tour (VCT), it features regional leagues leading to international championships.

STADIUM

Valorant events are hosted in large esports stadiums equipped with advanced lighting, massive screens, and live audience seating. These venues create an exciting atmosphere for both players and fans during major tournaments like the Valorant Champions Tour.
