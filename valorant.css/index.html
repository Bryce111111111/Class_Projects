ABOUT VALORANT

Valorant is a free-to-play first-person shooter developed by Riot Games. It features two teams of five players competing in tactical, round-based matches. The main game mode revolves around one team planting a device called the spike, while the other team tries to stop them. Players choose from a roster of agents, each with unique abilities that support different playstyles. The game emphasizes precise aim, strategy, and team coordination. Economy management plays a key role, as players buy weapons and abilities at the start of each round. Valorant also has a growing esports scene with international competitions. Its mix of gunplay and character abilities sets it apart from other shooters.

DOWNLOAD

Website

To install Valorant, visit the website, click “Play Free,” and sign in or create a Riot Games account. Download and run the installer, which sets up both Valorant and the anti-cheat system, Vanguard. After installation and a required restart, open the game, log in, and start playing.
